Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account and use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and password (stored as a secure hash)
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter
  • Basic usage data — pages visited and actions taken, used only to improve the service

We do not collect payment information. Spendly is a free tool and does not process any financial transactions on your behalf.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service:

  • To authenticate you and secure your account
  • To display your expense history and summaries
  • To identify and fix bugs or performance issues

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the service. We do not sell or rent your personal information to any third party.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on a secure server. Passwords are never stored in plain text — they are hashed using a one-way algorithm before being saved.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your expense records and personal information will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to operate:

  • Google Fonts — used to load the DM Sans and DM Serif Display typefaces. Google may log font requests in accordance with their own privacy policy.

We do not integrate with any analytics platforms, advertising networks, or social media trackers. No third party has access to your expense data.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you at any time. To exercise these rights, log into your account settings or contact us directly.

If you are located in the European Union, you may also have rights under the GDPR, including the right to data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the app's support channel. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
